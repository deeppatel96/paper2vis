1. Hierarchical Layout & Module Extraction
Instead of treating a paper as a flat text file, new systems like SciFig and FigAgent use a "hierarchical generation strategy".

The Workflow: One agent parses the manuscript to identify functional modules (e.g., "Encoder," "Loss Function"), while another agent assigns specific "canvas regions" to these concepts.

Why it's promising: It avoids the "spaghetti arrow" problem by generating logical inter-module connections rather than arbitrary point-to-point lines.
